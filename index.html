Births per 1000 Population by State

This map highlights the birth rates across different states in Malaysia, measured per 1,000 people. The darker shades indicate a higher number of births. Terengganu has the highest birth rate, while Kuala Lumpur and Penang exhibit the lowest rates.

Deaths per 1000 Population by State

This map represents the death rates across Malaysian states per 1,000 people. The darker areas indicate higher death rates, with Perak standing out as having the highest death rate. Selangor and Sabah have the lowest death rates.

Percentage of Births by State

This chart represents the distribution of births across different states in Malaysia. Each segment of the chart indicates the proportion of total births that occurred in each state, showcasing regional differences in birth rates. Selangor has the highest percentage of births, while Labuan has the lowest in Year 2022.

Percentage of Deaths by State

This chart illustrates the distribution of deaths by state in Malaysia. The segments represent the percentage of total deaths recorded in each state, showcasing regional differences in birth rates. Selangor has the highest percentage of deaths, while Putrajaya has the lowest in Year 2022.

Ten Years Back in Time from 2013 to 2022

Total Births in each State

This heatmap illustrates the total number of births in each state of Malaysia over the past ten years. The darker shades indicate a higher number of births. Putrajaya had the highest number of births per 1000 population in 2013, while KL and Penang had the lowest number of births per 1000 population in 2021.

Total Deaths in each State

This heatmap illustrates the total number of deaths in each state of Malaysia over the past ten years. The darker shades indicate a higher number of deaths. The highest number of deaths recorded was 9 per 1000 population, while Putrajaya had the lowest number of deaths at only 2 per 1000 population in 2016 and 2020.

Birth and Death Rates in Each State

The line charts in small multiples show trends in birth and death rates across Malaysian states from 2013 to 2022 .

Birth rates in most states show a downward trend, indicating fewer annual births, while death rates display a slightly increasing pattern, remaining much lower than birth rates overall.

Birth rates in Selangor are the highest, particularly in recent years, while states like Labuan and Perlis show the lowest birth rates throughout the period. This distinction highlights demographic differences between states, with larger states like Selangor has a higher number of annual births compared to smaller states like Labuan and Perlis.

In terms of death rates, the trend is relatively consistent across all states, showing minor increases over the ten-year period. Selangor records some of the highest death rates, which correlates with its larger population compared to other states. Meanwhile, Labuan and Putrajaya have consistently low death rates, reflecting their smaller populations.
